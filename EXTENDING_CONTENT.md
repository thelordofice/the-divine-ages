# Medieval RPG - Gameplay Guide

## Complete Content Breakdown (50+ Hours)

### Main Story (25-30 Hours)

**Act 1: The Awakening (5-7 Hours)**
- Tutorial and character creation
- Starting town quests (8 quests)
- First dungeon exploration
- Boss encounter: Goblin King
- Quest reward: 500 XP + Equipment

**Act 2: The Shadow Grows (6-8 Hours)**
- Kingdom exploration (12 quests)
- Castle infiltration
- NPC relationship building
- Mid-game boss: Dark Knight
- Quest reward: 2000 XP + Rare gear

**Act 3: Forbidden Lands (5-7 Hours)**
- Mountain region unlock
- Underground dungeon (10 quests)
- Faction introduction
- Major twist in story
- Quest reward: 5000 XP + Spells

**Act 4: The Convergence (5-7 Hours)**
- All regions converge on final quest
- 15 challenging quests
- NPC climax events
- Multiple ending paths
- Quest reward: 10000 XP + Legendary items

**Act 5: Post-Game Content (3-4 Hours)**
- New Game+ challenges
- Secret dungeon unlock
- True ending access
- Endgame quests (8 quests)
- Final rewards: 50000 XP

### Side Content (20-30 Hours)

**Side Quests (60+ Quests)**
- Town reputation quests (10-15 per town, 5 towns)
- Faction missions (5-10 per faction, 10 factions)
- NPC personal quests (3-5 per major NPC, 20+ NPCs)
- Hidden secret quests (10-15)
- Collectible hunts (10-15)
- Repeatable daily quests (unlimited)

**Dungeon Exploration (10-15 Hours)**
- 20 unique dungeons with layouts
- Each dungeon: 30 minutes to 2 hours
- Boss encounters with unique strategies
- Rare item drops from bosses
- Secret areas with hidden rewards

**Crafting & Professions (5-8 Hours)**
- Master 6 different professions
- Complete 200+ recipes
- Unlock rare item combinations
- Achieve crafting achievements
- Build ultimate equipment

**NPC Relationships & Romance (5-10 Hours)**
- Build reputation with 500 NPCs
- Complete romance storylines (5-10)
- Personal character arcs
- Marriage systems with NPCs
- Family/household management

**Achievement Hunting (3-5 Hours)**
- 100+ achievements across all categories
- Combat achievements (10 kills per enemy type)
- Exploration achievements (visit all locations)
- Social achievements (max reputation with all factions)
- Challenge achievements (beat tough boss fights)

### Combat Progression

**Early Game (Levels 1-20)**
- Basic attacks and 2-hit combos
- First spell unlocks (4 spells)
- Common equipment
- 4 dungeons available
- Difficulty: Easy to Normal

**Mid Game (Levels 20-60)**
- Advanced combos (3-4 hits)
- 20+ spells learned
- Rare and Epic equipment
- 12 dungeons available
- Faction wars begin
- Difficulty: Normal to Hard

**Late Game (Levels 60-100)**
- Master combos with special effects
- 50+ spells and synergies
- Legendary equipment farming
- 20+ dungeons available
- Faction control battles
- Difficulty: Hard to Nightmare

**Endgame (Post-100)**
- Infinite scaling difficulty
- Challenge dungeons
- PvP arena battles
- Secret boss encounters
- Unique prestige challenges

### Spell Synergies

**Fire + Frost = Plasma**
- Deals 3x normal damage
- Stuns enemies briefly
- Chains to nearby enemies

**Fire + Lightning = Inferno Storm**
- 5x normal damage
- Large AoE radius
- Continuous damage effect

**Frost + Lightning = Absolute Zero**
- Freezes enemies permanently
- Spreads to nearby enemies
- Creates hazardous terrain

**Death + Life = Balance**
- Heals and damages simultaneously
- Unique battlefield effects
- Powerful crowd control

### Equipment & Rarities

**Common (Gray) - Level 1-10**
- 50 different items
- Basic stat bonuses
- 1 slot for enchantment
- Value: 1-100 gold

**Uncommon (Green) - Level 11-25**
- 100 different items
- Moderate stat bonuses
- 2 slots for enchantments
- Value: 100-500 gold

**Rare (Blue) - Level 26-50**
- 150 different items
- Good stat bonuses

- Value: 500-5000 gold

**Epic (Purple) - Level 51-80**
- 100 different items
- Excellent stat bonuses

- Value: 5000-50000 gold

**Legendary (Gold) - Level 81-100**

### Crafting System

**Blacksmithing (40 Recipes)**
- Iron Sword → Steel Sword (10 ore + 5 leather)
- Plate Armor → Enchanted Plate (15 ore + 20 leather)
- Legendary Weapons (50 ore + 100 leather + rare drops)

**Alchemy (40 Recipes)**
- Health Potion (1 herb + 1 vial)
- Mana Potion (2 herbs + 1 vial + 1 crystal)
- Elixirs and Buffs (5+ herbs + reagents)

**Enchanting (40 Recipes)**
- Stat bonuses (+5-50 to any stat)
- Special effects (fire damage, lifesteal, etc.)
- Resistance enchants (50% damage reduction)

**Cooking (40 Recipes)**
- Basic Food (+10 HP regeneration)
- Gourmet Meals (+50 to all stats for 1 hour)
- Buff Foods (+100% experience gain)

**Herbalism (40 Recipes)**
- Passive gathering of herbs
- Rare plant locations
- High-yield gathering spots

### Difficulty Scaling

**Easy Mode**
- 50% enemy health
- 50% damage taken
- Double loot drops
- Quest markers always active
- Great for story/beginners

**Normal Mode (Default)**
- Standard difficulty
- 100% loot drops
- Quest markers on request
- Recommended for first playthrough

**Hard Mode**
- 150% enemy health
- 150% damage taken
- Double resource costs
- Limited healing
- For experienced players

**Legendary Mode**
- 200% enemy health
- 200% damage taken
- 3x resource costs
- Permadeath option available
- Only for veterans

**Nightmare Mode (Unlock at Level 100)**
- Infinite scaling difficulty
- Unique boss mechanics
- Exclusive rare loot
- True challenge experience
